Human or Machine

Due to war and famine humanity has declined. The last bastion of man decide to take their chance at sea.

The remaining 500 people (and you) are on two boats. 300 people are on one boat and 200 people are on the other. Each boat is already at max capacity.


There is a leak in both boats. You are the only mechanic that can fix the leak but you only have enough time to save one. Which do you choose?


Save 300 or Save 200
